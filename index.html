Our Heritage
A FAMILY'S
PROMISE

Samala Cloth Stores was born from a simple belief — that every woman deserves to wear silk that tells a story. Founded in Venkatagiri, the town that gave the world its most celebrated cotton and silk weaves, we have been the trusted choice of families across Andhra Pradesh for generations.

"In every thread we weave, lives the pride of Venkatagiri and the love we carry for our craft."

From a young bride choosing her wedding silk to a grandmother picking her festival cotton — we have been part of every milestone.
